# References to other media #

I'm sorry to say that not everything in the mod was invented my me. So here's the full list of references included in the mod.

- Field Charger: a reusable copy of Abiotic Factor's Field Charger
- Halt Shield: shield version of Door's Halt monster (which requires turning around to escape)
- Eldritch Shield: Terraria's Shield of Cthulhu but with damage boost on block instead of dashes
- Sticky Die, D-Ice: inspired by Mario Party's dice
- D10: JoJo's Bizarre Adventure's first antagonist, Dio
- Silver Bat: OFF's Batter's weapon
- Office Key/Handgun: Problem Sleuth's office key/handgun
- Leader's Sword: inspired by Oceanfalls' Nino (sword and healing ability)
- None of Your Business: the privacy advocacy group from Austria (which is where the color and lock come from)
- Radbow: SpaceStation 13's Radbow
- Yin-Yang Orb: Touhou
- SburbDB: SteamDB but Sburb
- Staff of Yggdrasil: inspired by Overlord
- Cosmic Plague related items: inspired by Dodecadragons' endgame section of the same name
- Donut: Voices of the Void has donuts on the floor that teleport the player to base when eaten
- Boondollars for Idiots: similar to the For Dummies books

## Genshin Impact ##

- Blank/Dull/Aspect Visions: aspect versions of visions. Names are based on ancient greek translations for their respective aspects (like Teyvat's elements)
- Saleswoman Glasses: glasses worn by Dori
- Divine/Mortal Temptation: one food item is called Adeptus' Temptation, which boosts damage

## Deltarune ##

- Bell Hammer: Gerson's hammer
- Blind Hammer: Sound of Justice's hammer
- Salesman Goggles: Spamton's goggles
- Dark Knight armor set: Inspired by the Roaring Knight

## Team Fortress 2 ##

- Golden Pan: one of the rarest and most expensive weapon (except made of gold instead of australium)
- Crusader's Crossbolt: Crusader's Crossbow but as a bolt
- Rocket Jump: using rockets (and other explosives) is an advanced mode of locomotion

## Honkai: Star Rail ##

- Destruction's Bat: Destruction Trailblazer's weapon. Healing not included
- Sun Reaver, New Moon: weapons from the Flame Reaver

## The Battle Cats ##

- Normal Cat Plush: a plush of the normal cat's basic form
- Cursed/Blessed Cat Staff: staves wielded by either Kasli fest variants
